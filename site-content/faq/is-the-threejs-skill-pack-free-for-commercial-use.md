## What the repository license permits

The root [ISC license](https://github.com/linegel/threejs-complete-set-of-skill/blob/main/LICENSE) covers repository-authored work and permits use, copying, modification, and distribution for any purpose, including paid and internal commercial work. It does not require publishing an application's source code merely because the application used these skill files as guidance.

The condition is short but material: copies must retain the copyright notice and permission notice. The license also disclaims warranties and liability. Free access to the repository does not create a support agreement or a promise that a generated implementation is fit for a particular commercial purpose.

## Incorporated work and separate costs

The repository incorporates Object Sculptor material under the [MIT notice carried with that skill](https://github.com/linegel/threejs-complete-set-of-skill/blob/main/skills/threejs-object-sculptor/LICENSE). Preserve that notice when copying or distributing affected files.

The repository license does not determine the terms or prices of:

- Three.js application assets, fonts, models, textures, audio, or datasets;
- third-party packages and plugins;
- an AI agent subscription or API;
- build infrastructure, hosting, monitoring, or storage;
- engineering, validation, accessibility, security, or support work.

The [pricing page](/pricing/) separates the pack's $0 license price from those real project costs. [Installation](/docs/install/) explains what the repository provides, while [agent-skill alternatives](/alternatives/threejs-agent-skills/) covers other approaches rather than invented paid tiers.

## Conditions and limitations

- Review the root ISC license and the incorporated Object Sculptor MIT notice in the revision you use.
- Check every third-party asset and dependency separately.
- The ISC license is provided as-is and does not include a warranty.
- This page states the repository's published terms; it is not legal advice for a particular transaction or jurisdiction.
